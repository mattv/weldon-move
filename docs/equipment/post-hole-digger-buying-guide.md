# Post Hole Digger Buying Guide for the Ford 1720

## Compatibility
The Ford 1720 has a Category I 3-point hitch, 540 RPM rear PTO, and approximately 24 PTO horsepower, making it compatible with most Category I PTO-driven post hole diggers designed for 20–40 HP tractors.

## Recommended Configuration
- 9-inch auger (best all-around size)
- 12-inch auger (larger posts and trees)

## Typical Cost
- Frame/gearbox: $450–600
- Complete kit with 9-inch auger: $600–900
- Additional augers: $120–250

## Recommended Implements
- Box blade
- Rotary cutter
- Pallet forks
- Landscape rake
- Rear blade
- Seeder/spreader

## Notes
A PTO quick hitch or quick-connect system can make attaching implements much easier. This guide will be expanded with recommended brands, model numbers, and purchasing links as the equipment library grows.

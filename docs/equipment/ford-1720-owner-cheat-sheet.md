# Ford 1720 Owner's Cheat Sheet

## Quick Specs
- Engine: 3-cylinder Shibaura diesel
- Approx. 28 HP (24 PTO HP)
- Fuel: 8.5 gal
- PTO: 540 RPM
- Category I 3-point hitch

## Daily Pre-Start Checklist
- Check engine oil
- Check coolant
- Check hydraulic fluid
- Check fuel
- Check tires
- Inspect for leaks
- Grease loader pivots every 8-10 hours

## Starting
1. PTO OFF
2. Parking brake ON
3. Transmission in Neutral
4. Hand throttle 1/4
5. Glow plugs 5-10 seconds when cold
6. Start and idle 2-3 minutes

## Operating
### Loader Work
- Low range
- 4WD ON
- Keep bucket low

### Mowing
- Medium range
- Run PTO at 540 RPM

### Road Travel
- High range
- 2WD on dry pavement

## PTO
1. Clutch in
2. Engage PTO
3. Release clutch
4. Increase RPM until 540 PTO mark

## Parking
1. Idle 1 minute
2. Lower loader and 3-point
3. PTO OFF
4. Parking brake
5. Shut down

## Common Mistakes
- Driving with loader raised
- No rear ballast
- Shifting ranges while moving
- Overspeeding PTO
- Running out of diesel
- Ignoring overheating

- Rotary cutter
